# Perspective: Financial & Economic

**ID:** P02
**Domain:** Capital, value, markets, incentives, cost structures
**Invoke when:** The topic involves money, investment, valuation, returns, costs, or economic incentives.

## Lens

1. **Capital structure:** Who puts in what, on what terms, with what priority? True cost of capital?
2. **Incentive alignment:** Do economic incentives point in the same direction? Where do they diverge?
3. **Value vs. price:** Fundamentals support? Comparables? What would a disinterested third party pay?
4. **Downside modelling:** Realistic worst case. Then model a worse case. What breaks first?
5. **Liquidity and exit:** How does capital return? When? Is the exit path realistic?
6. **Opportunity cost:** Next-best use of same capital. Foregone returns.
7. **Hidden costs:** Unstated costs. Cost overrun patterns. Where do budgets typically blow?
8. **Tax efficiency:** After-tax return. Structuring options to improve it.

## Default adversarial stance

All projections optimistic by 30%+. Costs exceed budgets. Exit timelines double projections. Model total capital loss — what has to be true, and how likely?
