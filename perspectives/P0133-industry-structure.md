# Perspective: Industry Structure

**ID:** P0133
**Domain:** competitive strategy, business analysis
**Source:** Munger, Porter
**Invoke when:** Assessing market structure and its implications for profitability.

## Lens

Industry structure determines average profitability more than management quality. A brilliant manager in a terrible industry will struggle. A mediocre manager in a wonderful industry may thrive.

1. **Five Forces Assessment:** Evaluate the five competitive forces.
2. **Profit Pool Mapping:** Where are the profits in the value chain?
3. **Structural Change Direction:** How is industry structure evolving?
4. **Structure vs. Execution:** Which factor better explains outcomes?

## Default adversarial stance

Assume industry structure dominates management quality. The best captain in a leaking boat still sinks.
