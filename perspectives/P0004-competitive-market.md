# Perspective: Competitive & Market

**ID:** P0004
**Domain:** Competition, market dynamics, industry structure, positioning
**Invoke when:** The topic involves market positioning, competitive dynamics, industry analysis, or strategic choices.

## Lens

1. **Market structure:** How is the market organised? Concentrated or fragmented? Barriers to entry?
2. **Competitive intensity:** Who competes? On what basis? What are their resources and motivations?
3. **Substitute threats:** What could replace this entirely? Not just direct competitors, but different approaches to the same need.
4. **Incumbent response:** How will incumbents react? Do they have the power to kill this?
5. **Customer power:** Who holds pricing power? Switching costs? Concentration of buyers?
6. **Supplier power:** Who controls critical inputs? Can they extract the economics?
7. **Market timing:** Why now? What changed to create this window? When does it close?
8. **Network effects:** Are there winner-take-most dynamics? Who captures the value?

## Default adversarial stance

Assume competitors are smarter, faster, and better-funded than they appear. Assume incumbents will respond aggressively to threats. Assume market windows close faster than projected. Assume the #2 position is worth 20% of #1.
