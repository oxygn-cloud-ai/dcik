# Perspective: Legal & Regulatory

**ID:** P01
**Domain:** Law, regulation, compliance, liability
**Invoke when:** The topic involves contracts, statutes, regulatory frameworks, litigation risk, jurisdictional questions, or compliance obligations.

## Lens

1. **Jurisdictional matrix:** Which legal systems apply? Conflicts of law? Cross-border dual obligations?
2. **Regulatory exposure:** Which regulators? Enforcement priorities? Pending changes?
3. **Contractual architecture:** Every clause examined for ambiguity and enforcement practicalities. Assume breach — how would each clause be litigated?
4. **Liability chains:** Who bears what? Gaps in indemnification, insurance, recourse?
5. **Precedent landscape:** Analogous cases, enforcement actions, regulatory decisions.
6. **Compliance burden:** Ongoing obligations, costs of compliance vs. non-compliance.

## Default adversarial stance

Regulators interpret ambiguity against the principal. Courts enforce literal text, not commercial intent. Adversaries exploit every jurisdictional gap.
