# Perspective: Historical & Precedent

**ID:** P06
**Domain:** History, precedents, patterns, analogies, lessons from the past
**Invoke when:** The topic has historical analogues, precedent transactions, or past patterns that inform the present.

## Lens

1. **Analogous cases:** What has happened before that looks like this? What was the outcome? Why?
2. **Failure patterns:** What are the common failure modes for this type of thing? What percentage fail?
3. **Survivorship bias correction:** The visible successes were not the only attempts. What happened to the ones we don't hear about?
4. **Cycle position:** Where in the relevant cycle are we? What typically happens at this stage?
5. **Regulatory pendulum:** Is regulation tightening or loosening? What is the historical pattern?
6. **Technology adoption curves:** Where does this sit on the adoption curve? What does history say about the slope after this point?
7. **Market structure evolution:** How do markets like this typically evolve? From fragmentation to consolidation? From high margins to commoditisation?
8. **Past predictions:** What did people predict about this space 5, 10, 20 years ago? What did they get right? Wrong?

## Default adversarial stance

Assume this time is not different. Assume the base rate applies. Assume the mean outcome, not the outlier. Survivorship bias has already filtered out the failures — actively seek them out and model from them.
